One-stop shop
solution for confectionery

Gum Base is an insoluble,inert,and non-nutritive masticatory substance used to
manufacture chewing and bubble gum.It is what gives the candy its consistency
and what allows the addition of different ingredients such as sugar,glucose,
polyols. At shriji Gumbase, we manufacture over 206 different Gum Base compositions for the Gum Candy industry worldwide. We are your One-stop bubble gum factory.
